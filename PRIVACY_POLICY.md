# Privacy Policy for YouTube Comment Picker

**Last Updated: December 9, 2025**

## Overview

YouTube Comment Picker ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles data.

## Data Collection

**The Extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Data Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your device:

### What the Extension Accesses:
- **YouTube Comments**: The Extension reads publicly visible comments on YouTube pages when you activate it
- **Session Data**: Comments and filters are temporarily stored in your browser's local storage

### What Happens to Your Data:
- **Stored Locally**: All extracted comments are stored only in Chrome's local storage on your device
- **No External Servers**: No data is sent to external servers or third parties
- **No Analytics**: We do not collect usage statistics or analytics
- **No Tracking**: We do not track your browsing behavior

## Permissions

The Extension requires these permissions:

- **activeTab**: To access the current YouTube page content
- **scripting**: To extract comments from the page DOM
- **storage**: To save session data locally in your browser

## Data Retention

- Session data persists until you:
  - Navigate to a different YouTube page
  - Click "Clear Session"
  - Uninstall the Extension
- You have complete control over your data at all times

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- Clear all stored data at any time using the "Clear Session" button
- Uninstall the Extension to remove all stored data
- Request information about data handling (contact below)
